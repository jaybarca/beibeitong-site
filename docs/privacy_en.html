Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 4, 2026

The developer takes your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how BeiBeiTong ("the App") handles information while you use it.

1. We Do Not Collect Personal Information

The App does not require account registration or login to use any of its features.

The App does not collect, store, transmit, or share any information that could identify you personally, including but not limited to your name, phone number, email address, or location.

2. Local Data Storage

The App operates on local storage. Your learning records, progress, and preference settings are stored only on your device and are never uploaded to the developer's servers or any third-party server.

The developer cannot access, view, or recover any data stored on your device. If you delete the App, clear its data, or switch devices, this data may be permanently lost. Please back up any information you wish to keep.

3. Third-Party Services

The App does not currently integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or statistics services, and does not share user data with any third party.

If a future version integrates such services (for example, crash reporting or anonymous usage statistics), this policy will be updated accordingly, and your consent will be requested where required by law.

4. Children's Privacy

The App is suitable for learners of all ages. Because the App does not collect any personal information, it does not pose a risk of collecting or disclosing personal information from users of any age, including children.

5. Data Security

Because the App does not collect or transmit personal information, there is no risk of data breach arising from network transmission or server-side storage. The security of data stored locally on your device depends on your device's own security settings.

6. Your Rights

As the App does not collect any personal information, requests to access, correct, or delete personal data generally do not apply. If you have any questions about this, please feel free to contact the developer.

7. Changes to This Policy

The developer may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect product updates, legal requirements, or business needs.

Any revised version will become effective once published on the App's official website or related pages. Material changes will be highlighted where appropriate.

8. Contact

If you have any questions, comments, or suggestions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer through the Support page.
