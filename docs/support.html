常见问题

本应用需要联网使用吗？
不需要。贝背通是一款离线学习应用，安装完成后无需网络连接即可使用全部功能。

我的学习记录会同步到其他设备吗？
目前不会。所有学习数据仅保存在本设备本地，暂不支持跨设备同步。

卸载应用后学习记录还能找回吗？
无法找回。由于数据仅存储在本地，卸载或清除应用数据后相关记录将无法恢复，请注意提前留意。

Frequently Asked Questions

Does the App require an internet connection?
No. BeiBeiTong is an offline learning app — once installed, all features work without a network connection.

Does my learning progress sync across devices?
Not currently. All data is stored locally on your device only, and cross-device sync is not yet supported.

Can I recover my progress after deleting the App?
No. Since data is stored locally only, uninstalling the App or clearing its data will permanently erase your progress.
